Creatives' Parlour

Arts for Social Change

Creatives Parlour is a global space where we identify, nurture and celebrate creative talents in young people from all spheres of life with an aim of making use of our creative talents and capabilities to make a positive impact in society.

Mission

To apply art, innovation and technology in solving societal problems.

Core Values

Innovation, Creativity, Self-Improvement, Disruption

Vision

To unlock every creative potential for development of the self and our surroundings.

About Us

Creatives Parlour recognizes the place of popular Creative arts in the larger global developmental challenges and discourses. We utilize talent in the creation of meaningful discussions of significance in society such as gender equity, Women’s rights, public health, Mental health, Climate change and mitigation of its adverse effects.

We produce and communicate these important issues through popular art forms, harnessing the power of creativity to address and illuminate critical societal topics such as:

Films for development, community theatre and theatre for development, digital media, online content creation, music, dance, poetry and all the fun things, but with development in mind.

Talent Rapture: Festival of the Creative Arts

In order to discover and nurture young talent, we host the annual “Talent Rapture: Festival of the Creative Arts”. This is a festival that brings together young Talented creatives in Kenya and beyond for a three-day celebration and recognition of the power of the Creative Arts in society and its development. Most people lose their creative vigour and confidence simply because their creative talent was not identified, acknowledged, nurtured and rewarded positively from an early age. Talent Rapture bridges this gap by bringing together private international curricula schools. To participate in our next festival write to: talentrapture@gmail.com

International Creative Arts Festivals

We appreciate the importance of interaction with like-minded and similarly skilled people in the creative spaces around and beyond Kenya. We therefore invite creative artists to lead our workshops and augment our performances for a blend that benefits young talent in Kenya. Thus, we identify, collate international festivals that local creatives can participate in to enhance mutual learning and benefits through creative performances and works. We thus connect local artists to the world.

Arts production for social change

At Creatives Parlour, we harness the existing creativity in society and mostly marginalized groups and unpack their social predicaments through the creative power of the arts. This opens up conversations that are healthy and therapeutic to these groups. We invite committed artists to work on stage and film productions that speak to issues that concern the society, both in Kenya and globally.

Workshops

Creatives Parlour organizes capacity building workshops through targeted, tailor-made one-on-one coaching in areas of:

Brand Visibility

Enhancing participants' ability to make their creative work more visible and recognizable.

Personal Branding

Guiding participants to build and maintain a personal brand aligning with their goals.

Public Speaking

Developing effective communication skills to articulate creative ideas confidently.

CV Writing

Providing guidance on crafting compelling CVs that highlight creative skills and experiences.

Presentation skills

Training on how to present ideas and work in a clear, engaging, and professional manner.

Professional etiquette

Teaching norms and behaviors expected in professional and creative environments.

Leadership skills

Fostering the ability to lead projects and inspire others within the creative sector.

Brand presence

Improving participants' ability to create and maintain a strong presence across platforms.

Job Interview Preparation

Preparing participants to succeed in job interviews, with a focus on showcasing their creative talents.
